Online Hands On Learning

San Diego Children's Discovery Museum

San Diego Children's Discovery Museum provides learning opportunities for families, students, and teachers to access from home. Join us every day for Art, World Culture, Music, Tinker Time and more! In addition, daily STREAM activities help families engage with Science, Technology, Reading, Engineering, Art, and Math concepts using household materials. 

All online hands on learning opportunities can be found for free at https://www.sdcdm.org/blog. 

Also, don't miss our live storytime everyday at 12:30pm on our Facebook page @sdcdm.
