Remake Learning Days Contest: Get your Gears Moving!

Brain Balance of San Diego

Get your gears moving with Brain Balance San Diego (BBSD) and The San Diego Automotive Museum (SDAM) by engineering your very own race vehicle in celebration of Remake Learning Days. Register for a complimentary S.T.E.A.M. (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, Math) kit to design, build, and compete how far your vehicle can go.

Ignite your child’s creativity, imagination, and planning skills which are critical for fostering brain development and connectivity and cultivate important 21st century skills like teamwork and attention! S.T.E.A.M. Vehicle Contest*:

  • Join us Sunday, May 1, 2022 from 10:30 AM - 12:30 PM at the San Diego Automotive Museum to test and race your vehicles!
  • Contestants will be judged on the creativity and innovation of their vehicle, speed, and travel distance in their respective age groups (5-8, 9-11, 12-14).
  • All winners will receive a special prize and have a chance to display their vehicle at The San Diego Automotive Museum and win a free Brain Balance Comprehensive Assessment.
  • Winners will be announced May 2, 2022.

Registration required. Limited supplies. Free for all ages; best for ages 5-14. Limit 1 kit per child.

Unregistered, walk-up contestants may compete while supplies last. Contestants will have free admission into the Museum after the race.

Registrants may pick up their S.T.E.A.M. kits at either Brain Balance of San Diego in Carmel Mountain Ranch (10175 Rancho Carmel Dr., Suite 124, San Diego, CA 92128) or The San Diego Automotive Museum in Balboa Park (2080 Pan American Plaza, San Diego, CA 92101) between April 11th-30th. Select your preferred pick-up location in your registration.

*Disclaimer: Contest is valid for Brain Balance of San Diego and The San Diego Automotive Museum only. Brain Balance Centers are individually owned and operated. This contest is in no way affiliated with any other Brain Balance Center location. Contest begins May 1, 2022 from 10:30 AM to 12:00 PM PST. Winners will be contacted May 2, 2022. Prize value is $5-$10. Each San Diego Automotive Museum ticket value is up to $14.50. Free Brain Balance assessment value is $195. No purchase necessary.
