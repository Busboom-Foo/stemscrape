Why we build a STEM Ecosystem

San Diego as a STEM-rich region needs to be ready for the challenges and opportunities of tomorrow.

The next generation must be equipped to become the thinkers, problem solvers and innovators we need today and in the future. We want all learners have a chance to be immersed in Science, Technology, Engineering and Math—to make our world a better place.
